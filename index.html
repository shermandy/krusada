Offering a new future for ex-addicts on Bonaire

Who is Krusada?

For more than 15 years, Krusada has been focusing on people who are unable to keep up with the society of Bonaire including those that struggle with addiction, a criminal past or people with poor social skills. Krusada is a place of recovery that provides a Christian vision.

We follow the parable of the Good Samaritan who did not pass by a neighbour in need.

What is the problem?

Housing in Bonaire is expensive, overcrowded, and substandard. Utility prices are often exorbitant. Further, it does not meet the needs of people in crisis that we serve.

How is Krusada helping?

Krusada offers housing to people that are a threat to the wellbeing of the communities in which they reside. Through our programs, we seek to advance prosperity on Bonaire and to improve the social cohesion between our clients and the local residents.

Rebuilding a substance free, structured life is a long-term project. Living at Krusada focuses on self-support with only minimal in-house care.

How can YOU help?

Krusada wants to help the needy by building a unit of 8 apartments that share a communal space. The apartments will provide private living space for their clients and space for meetings.

Each apartment building will cost approximatetly $30,000. Donations are greatly needed to either fund the general construction of the building or individual items including furniture and appliances.
